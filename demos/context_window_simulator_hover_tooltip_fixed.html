Key Takeaway: Every conversation has a finite window. The AI holds your messages and its own responses in that same space. Complex exchanges fill it faster. Once full, the AI loses your earlier context — which is why it seems to "forget."

This is why shorter, focused conversations often work better than one massive thread — and why clear, specific prompts matter.
