### Workshop Overview
Artificial Intelligence is no longer a future concept—it's a set of practical tools already embedded in everyday products and business workflows. This talk offers a clear, non-hyped overview of what AI is (and isn't), how it evolved to today's machine learning and generative AI, and what that shift means for organizations right now.

Using a handful of short, concrete examples from multiple industries, we'll look at where AI reliably adds value (e.g., customer service, document analysis, workflow automation) and where caution is still needed. We'll also discuss how to choose use cases that are feasible, measurable, and worth the effort. We'll close with practical recommendations, common risks and ethical considerations such as privacy, security, and workforce impacts.

### Key Topics

- Machine learning vs. Generative AI
- Practical applications for organizations
- Workflow automation and document analysis
- Customer service enhancement
- Risk assessment and ethical considerations
- Privacy and security implications
